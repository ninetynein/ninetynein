Tales from a Reluctant Sailor

Hide from the Tide - Tales from a Reluctant Sailor

With a heavy heart I bid farewell to my good friend Jo. He was packing up his family, selling his condo, and moving back to Regina, Saskatchewan to be closer to his family. During the process of the move, Jo invited us down to his small little sailboat to kick back with a few beers. I jokingly suggested that I might be interested in taking it off his hands before he moved. Thinking it would be a non-starter with my wife, I mentioned it to her and another friend, only to find that they were fully onboard (pardon the...). So after chatting with Jo a bit more, we agreed on a (very low) price, and the boat and slip were ours!

My name is Tyson. I, along with two of my friends (one of which I happen to be married to) got ourselves a boat! Follow along as we start from scratch and jump head first into repairs, equipment, and sailing in general.
